The Perfect Milkshake

Description

Reminiscent of countertop service and carhop days, there is nothing better than a perfect milkshake. Luckily, making one at home is extremely easy. This easy milkshake recipe gives you the perfect ratio of milk to ice cream and is completely customizable!

Can I make it ahead of time?

Surprisingly, yes! Blend up your milkshake and pour it into your glass, then stick it back in the freezer. Just let it sit out for 5 minutes or so before it's drinkable again! It&apos,s a fantastic party hack.

What are good mix-ins?

Oooh, our favorite question! The mix-ins are endless. You can add Oreos, Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, brownies, cookie dough, chocolate syrup, or fruit. Anything you like! Add peanut butter, bananas, and chocolate chips for a Chunky Monkey vibe, or caramel, coconut, and chocolate chips for a Samoas-like milkshake. Or go all out with wine for a Boozy Red Wine Milkshake.

If you don't want your mix-ins completely blended, like brownies or cookie dough, stir them in after blending the ice cream and milk.

Can i make a milkshake without a blender?

Yes! And it's easier than you think! Add your ice cream and milk to large bowl and use a rubber spatula to stir until the milk is incorporated and then pour into a glass. Simple!

How do I turn it into malt?

I love malts and think they are far superior to milkshakes. You can turn your milkshake into a malt by adding 2 tablespoons malt powder to your ice cream and milk and blend them altogether!

Ingredients

For the best Vanilla Milkshake:

  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 c. millk
  • Whipped topping, for garnish
  • Sprinkles, for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish

      • Steps

      1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
      2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
